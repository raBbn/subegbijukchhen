About Me

A geologist with a PhD in strong-motion seismology. I’m currently involved in teaching and research as Associate Professor of Khwopa Engineering College and Khwopa College of Engineering, colleges undertaken by Bhaktapur Municipality in Nepal. My research interests are seismology, disaster management, remote sensing, and geoscience. I love to travel (well.. who doesn’t) and interested in astronomy, photography, music, movies, and sports.
